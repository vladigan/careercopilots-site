How It Works

Three steps. Then it runs itself.

Set it up once. Your co-pilot handles the repetitive part of the job search from there.

1

Set your filters

Tell the co-pilot your target titles, locations, salary floor, and remote preference. A 20-minute setup — then you're done.

2

It applies 24/7

The bot scores every matched LinkedIn Easy Apply role, fills the form, answers the screening questions, and submits — while you sleep.

3

You interview

Wake up to a pipeline instead of a blank inbox. Your only job is responding to recruiters and prepping for the calls that matter.
