Career Copilots

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. What We Collect

We collect: • Account information (name, email, password hash — never plaintext) • Profile data you provide (phone, address, career preferences, resume) • Job application activity (jobs discovered, applied, scores) • Agent session metadata (version, OS, last ping time) • Payment information processed securely by Stripe — we never see your card number

2. How We Use It

Your data is used exclusively to: • Operate the Career Copilots service and fill out job applications on your behalf • Send you transactional emails (verification, password reset, application milestones) • Improve job scoring and matching algorithms (aggregated, never sold) • Provide customer support We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

3. LinkedIn & Third-Party Platforms

Career Copilots automates interactions with LinkedIn on your behalf, using your own browser session running on your own device. We never store your LinkedIn password. You are responsible for ensuring your use of this service complies with LinkedIn's Terms of Service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases hosted on Railway (SOC 2 compliant infrastructure). Your resume files are stored in isolated, access-controlled storage. API tokens are hashed with SHA-256 and never stored in plaintext. We use HTTPS everywhere.

5. Data Retention

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account. Upon deletion, all personal data, job history, and uploaded resumes are permanently removed within 30 days. Billing records are retained as required by law.

6. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: • Access all data we hold about you (email support@careercopilots.com) • Export your data in JSON format (Settings → Export Data) • Correct inaccurate data (Settings → Profile) • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Account) • Opt out of milestone notification emails (Settings → Notifications)

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@careercopilots.com
